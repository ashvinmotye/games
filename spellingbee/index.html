Guide

Spelling bee is a competition where you are given a word and you have to spell it correctly. In the actual competition, you are given the options of repeating the word, part of speech, definition, example in a sentence and language origin. Also, once you start spelling, you cannot correct yourself.

In my practice version, you are allowed:

  • 3 repeats of the word
  • 2 repeats of definition
  • 2 repeats of example

At the start of each round, you can press "ENTER" to say the word automatically. Alternatively, click the designated buttons.

There are 000 words in the game. For every 10 correct words, you move up a level. And, if you manage to complete all correctly, you will be awarded the Champions Cup of the game!

Good luck!
